The problem

As digital design is a young and broad field, the range of existing job titles is vast and inconsistent. For designers who want to enter this job market, it can be difficult to understand what positions they can fill and what titles they should adopt, given their skills. The challenge was to create a solution to help designers define their job title.

The solution

Untangler helps design students, professionals and recruiters to find job titles in the digital design field based on selected skills. It gathers relevant information about each position, allowing a better understanding of each job and easing the job search process or recruiting process. By organizing the titles by clusters, Untangler also contributes to synthesize the lexicon in this recent area. This solution was idealized to be used in the context of FBAUL.
